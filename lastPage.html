Dice are made by melting plastic pellets, injecting the molten plastic into molds, and then painting and packaging the dice.

  1. Prepare the material
  2. Melt the plastic
  3. Inject the plastic
  4. Cool the plastic
  5. Open the mold
  6. Paint the dice
  7. Tumble the dice
  8. Test the dice
